About Cryptobeam Fund

Investment Strategy

Cryptobeam Fund LP is a Delaware limited partnership that invests in digital assets, blockchain technologies, and cryptocurrency-related securities. Our fund operates under SEC Regulation D, Rule 506(b), limiting investments to accredited investors only.

Regulatory Compliance

Our fund adheres to all applicable securities laws and regulations:

  • Regulation D 506(b): Private placement exemption for accredited investors
  • Form D Filing: Filed with the SEC and applicable state securities regulators
  • Anti-Money Laundering: Comprehensive KYC/AML procedures
  • Investor Accreditation: Verification through Plaid and documentation review

Technology Stack

Our investor portal is built with security and compliance as top priorities:

  • Auth0: Enterprise-grade authentication and authorization
  • Firebase: Secure cloud data storage and real-time updates
  • DocuSign: Legally binding electronic signatures
  • Plaid: Financial data verification for accreditation
  • Webflow: Professional web design and hosting

Investor Requirements

To invest in Cryptobeam Fund, you must:

  • Be an accredited investor as defined by SEC Rule 501
  • Meet minimum investment threshold of $250,000
  • Complete investor onboarding and verification
  • Sign required legal documentation (NDA, LPA, Subscription Agreement)
