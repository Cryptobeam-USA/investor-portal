## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the parties desire to form a limited partnership pursuant to the Delaware Revised Uniform Limited Partnership Act (6 Del. C. § 17-101, et seq.) (the "Act") for the purpose of investing in digital assets, blockchain-related securities, and other investment opportunities;

**WHEREAS**, the General Partner will manage the business and affairs of the Partnership;

**WHEREAS**, the Limited Partners desire to invest in the Partnership subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

### 1.1 Formation

### 1.2 Name

### 1.5 Purpose

## ARTICLE II - CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

### 2.1 Initial Capital Contributions
Each Limited Partner shall make an initial Capital Contribution to the Partnership in the amount set forth on such Limited Partner's signature page or subscription agreement.

### 2.2 Minimum Investment
The minimum initial Capital Contribution for each Limited Partner shall be **$250,000 USD**, unless waived by the General Partner in its sole discretion.

### 2.3 Additional Capital Contributions
- Limited Partners shall not be required to make any additional Capital Contributions
- Limited Partners may make additional Capital Contributions only with the consent of the General Partner
- The General Partner may, but is not required to, make additional Capital Contributions

### 2.4 Return of Capital
No Partner shall have the right to withdraw or demand the return of its Capital Contribution except as specifically provided in this Agreement.

### 2.5 Interest on Capital
No interest shall be paid on any Capital Contribution.

---

## ARTICLE III - ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

### 3.1 Allocations of Profits and Losses
Except as otherwise provided in this Agreement:
- Net Profits and Net Losses shall be allocated among the Partners in accordance with their respective Partnership Percentages
- Partnership Percentage means, for each Partner, the ratio of such Partner's Capital Account to the aggregate Capital Accounts of all Partners

### 3.2 Distribution Waterfall
Distributions shall be made in the following order of priority:

**Tier 1 - Return of Capital:**
First, to the Limited Partners until each Limited Partner has received distributions equal to their Capital Contributions

**Tier 2 - Preferred Return:**
Second, to the Limited Partners until they have received a cumulative preferred return of [X]% per annum on their unreturned Capital Contributions

**Tier 3 - Catch-up:**
Third, [X]% to the General Partner until it has received [Y]% of aggregate distributions

**Tier 4 - Carried Interest:**
Thereafter, [X]% to the Limited Partners and [Y]% to the General Partner

### 3.3 Timing of Distributions
Distributions shall be made at such times as the General Partner determines in its sole discretion.

---

## ARTICLE IV - MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Management by General Partner
- The General Partner shall have full and exclusive authority to manage and control the business and affairs of the Partnership
- The General Partner may make all decisions affecting the business of the Partnership without the consent of the Limited Partners

### 4.2 Limited Partner Restrictions
Limited Partners shall not:
- Take part in the management or control of the Partnership business
- Have any authority to act for or bind the Partnership
- Participate in the conduct of the Partnership business

### 4.3 Powers of General Partner
The General Partner shall have all powers necessary or appropriate to carry out the purposes of the Partnership, including but not limited to:
- Making investments and dispositions of Partnership assets
- Hiring employees, consultants, and service providers
- Executing agreements on behalf of the Partnership
- Maintaining books and records
- Filing tax returns and regulatory filings

## ARTICLE IX - DISSOLUTION AND TERMINATION

### 9.1 Events of Dissolution
