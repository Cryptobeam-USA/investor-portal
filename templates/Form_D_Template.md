2. **When to File:** File no later than 15 calendar days after the first sale of securities in the offering
3. **Where to File:** File electronically through the EDGAR system
4. **Amendments:** File amendments to a Form D for the same offering annually and within 30 days when there are changes to information previously reported

### REGULATION D RULE 506(b) REQUIREMENTS

**General Requirements:**
- No general solicitation or advertising permitted
- Sales limited to accredited investors and up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors
- Investors must have access to or be provided with disclosure documents similar to those used in registered offerings
- Must file Form D within 15 days after first sale

**Accredited Investor Definition (SEC Rule 501):**
- Individual with net worth exceeding $1 million (excluding primary residence), OR
- Individual with income exceeding $200,000 in each of the two most recent years (or $300,000 joint income with spouse)
- Certain entities with assets exceeding $5 million
